Prime
A Manual Camera app built for simplicity

What is Prime?

Prime is a manual camera app built exclusively for the iPhone. It allows you to take full control of the iPhone's camera allowing you to shoot in many different conditions without having to worry about getting the perfect shot.

Prime takes its name from prime lenses(The iPhone uses a prime lens). That means that it has a fixed focal length.

Full manual control for:

- Shutter Speed
- ISO
- Exposure Compensation
- Focus
- White Balance

Other features

- Manual focus can be used in auto mode if needed
- Timer modes for: 2, 3, 5, and 10 seconds
- Flash modes: Off, Auto and On
- Live Shutter Speed, ISO
- All images saved directly to Camera Roll
- Switch between front and back camera
- Rule of thirds grid
